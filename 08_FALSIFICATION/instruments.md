## Data Sources Used for Validation

### ESO S-Star Data (Sgr A*)
- Objects: S2, S0-2 and other S-stars
- Instrument: VLTI/GRAVITY
- Use: Perihelion precession, redshift comparison
- Anti-circularity: Data NOT used for SSZ calibration

### NICER Neutron Star Data
- Objects: PSR J0030+0451, PSR J0740+6620, PSR J0348+0432
- Instrument: NICER on ISS
- Use: Mass-radius determination, redshift predictions

### GPS Time Measurement
- Source: US Naval Observatory, BIPM
- Measurement: Time difference ground ↔ satellite = ~45.85 μs/day
- Use: Weak-field validation (SSZ ≡ GR)

### Pound-Rebka Experiment
- Measurement: Gravitational redshift over 22.6 m height
- Expected: Δν/ν = 2.46 × 10⁻¹⁵
- Use: Weak-field validation (SSZ ≡ GR, <0.01% deviation)

### EHT Black Hole Shadow
- Objects: M87* (2019), Sgr A* (2022)
- Use: Shadow diameter comparison
- SSZ prediction: -1.3% smaller than GR

### Gaia Stellar Catalog (DR3, 2022)
- Use: Stellar parameters for validation

### Schumann Resonance Data
- Use: Exploratory scaling analysis (ssz-schumann repo)
