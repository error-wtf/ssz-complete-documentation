**Abstract**

holes, but applied to non-singular astrophysical systems.


Segmented Spacetime thus provides a physically grounded mechanism for the emergence of
molecular shells and dynamic asymmetries in expanding nebulae, based on temporal
stratification rather than external forcing or hidden mass components.
